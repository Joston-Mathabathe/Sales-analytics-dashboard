# Sales Performance Analysis – Superstore Dataset

## Project Overview

This project analyses retail sales data from a Superstore dataset using Python. The aim is to identify sales trends, evaluate regional performance, and generate business insights that can support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large amounts of transactional data every day. This project investigates sales and profit performance across different regions and products to identify opportunities for improving business performance.

---

## Objectives

- Analyse overall sales and profit performance
- Compare sales across different regions
- Identify the highest-selling products
- Generate business recommendations based on the analysis

---

## Key Findings

- The West region generated the highest sales.
- The South region recorded the lowest sales.
- High-value office equipment contributed significantly to total revenue.
- Overall profit was much lower than total sales, suggesting opportunities to improve profit margins.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Investigate the causes of weaker performance in the South region.
- Review discount strategies to improve profitability.
- Continue investing in high-performing regions.
- Focus on products that generate both strong sales and strong profit.
